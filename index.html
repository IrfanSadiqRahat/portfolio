About Me

As an ML researcher, I'm driven by the endless possibilities of algorithms and neural networks. Each line of code unravels new insights, pushing boundaries in AI and machine learning with relentless curiosity and determination. I have over 10 years of experience across web design, development and app creation, blending technical expertise with creative vision.

My journey has taken me from Dhaka to India, earning a B.Tech in Computer Science & Engineering with a focus on Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning. Along the way I've led technical clubs, organised workshops and collaborated on research projects in medical imaging and agriculture. I'm passionate about using technology to solve real‑world problems and always eager to learn more.
